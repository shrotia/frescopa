Our Journey

Fréscopa was born from a passion for exceptional coffee and the belief that every cup should be an experience. Founded in 2018 by coffee enthusiasts Sarah and Marcus, our journey began with a simple mission: to connect coffee lovers with the world's finest beans while supporting the farmers who grow them.

From the highlands of Ethiopia to the mountains of Colombia, we travel the world to source our beans directly from farmers who share our commitment to quality and sustainability. Every relationship we build is based on trust, fairness, and a shared love for the craft of coffee growing.

What started as a small roastery in San Francisco has grown into a community of coffee lovers who appreciate the artistry, science, and passion that goes into every cup. We believe that great coffee has the power to bring people together, create moments of joy, and make every day a little brighter.

🇪🇹 Ethiopia - Yirgacheffe

Altitude: 1,700-2,200m above sea level

Processing: Washed & Natural

Flavor Notes: Bright acidity, floral, citrus, wine-like

The birthplace of coffee, Ethiopian beans offer complex fruity and floral characteristics unique to this ancient coffee culture. We work directly with cooperatives in the Yirgacheffe region to bring you these exceptional beans.

🇨🇴 Colombia - Huila

Altitude: 1,200-2,000m above sea level

Processing: Fully Washed

Flavor Notes: Medium body, caramel, chocolate, nutty

From the Andes mountains, Colombian Supremo beans deliver consistently balanced flavor with rich chocolate undertones. Our partnership with local farmers ensures sustainable practices and fair wages.

🇬🇹 Guatemala - Antigua

Altitude: 1,500-1,700m above sea level

Processing: Fully Washed

Flavor Notes: Full body, spicy, smoky, wine-like acidity

Grown in volcanic soil around Antigua, these beans develop distinctive spicy and smoky characteristics with remarkable complexity. The volcanic ash enriches the soil, creating unique mineral profiles.
