Coffee Growing Regions

🇪🇹 Ethiopian Highlands

The birthplace of coffee, offering complex fruity and floral characteristics. High altitude farms produce beans with bright acidity and wine-like qualities.

🇨🇴 Colombian Andes

Renowned for consistent quality and balanced flavor profiles. The mountainous terrain and ideal climate produce full-bodied coffees with chocolate notes.

🇬🇹 Guatemala Volcanic Regions

Volcanic soil adds unique mineral complexity. High altitude and volcanic ash create distinctive spicy and smoky flavor profiles.
